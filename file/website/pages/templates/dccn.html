Data Communication and Computer Networks

Data communication and computer networks are fundamental aspects of modern technology, enabling the exchange of information between devices. Data communication refers to the process of transferring data between two or more devices, while computer networks are systems that facilitate this communication. These networks can range from simple connections like a local area network (LAN) to the vast expanse of the internet.
